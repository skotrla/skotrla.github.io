Escapes! Vacation Resorts

Today more than 100,000 families own property in one of the five Cooper communities located throughout the Mid-South. Why have they bought property from us? For several very important reasons. First is our outstanding reputation. We are widely recognized as one of the most fiscally responsible developers of planned communities in the nation. That means property owners can count on very high quality amenities now – and in the future. Next is our environmental concern. Fully 20% to 30% of the land in each Cooper community is set aside as unspoiled common property. As a result, Cooper communities feature lush, wooded landscapes and lots of strikingly beautiful scenery.

Another reason people are so attracted to Cooper communities is because of our ideal locations. From Arkansas and Tennessee to Missouri and South Carolina, we’ve chosen sites that are all just a short drive to attractions like national parks, world-class entertainment, top-flight shopping and more. And, since our villages are all in the Mid-South, the climate is usually just right – not too hot, and not too cold!

There are several companies under the Cooper Communities “umbrella.” They include Cooper Land Development, Inc. (the company that actually sells the land in most of the Cooper Villages). Then there’s Cooper Homes, which custom-builds homes with additional services other builders can’t provide. And Escapes! Resorts, which features vacation ownership in several of our villages.

Sure, Cooper Communities villages have all the golf, tennis, boating and fishing you could ask for, but we’ve designed our communities to be more than that. They’re places with real neighborhoods, where people raise families, and where every generation really can find a place to call… home.

 

Destinations

Escapes! to Bella Vista Village

Located in Northwest Arkansas, Bella Vista Village offers stunning natural beauty in the Ozark Mountains. Bella Vista’s 36,000 acres offers golf, tennis, fishing, swimming, and more. With eight 18-hole championship courses, one nine-hole course, and six pro shops, you are never more than a few minutes from a tee.


You can enjoy boating and fishing on eight lakes. These lakes are open to all Bella Vista owners and guests so you don’t have to worry about crowds. The lakes are stocked in order to provide the best in bass, crappie, and bream fishing. The Yacht Club and marina are located on the largest body of water, Loch Lomond.

Escapes! to Hot Springs Village

Nestled in Arkansas’s Ouachita Mountains and surrounded by vacation attractions, this resort’s natural beauty and man-made amenities truly offer something for everyone. Tee it up on your choice of eight outstanding courses that fan out over 153 holes. Enjoy private lakes, indoor and outdoor pools, country clubs, fitness/recreational centers and a system of scenic nature trails that extend over 30 miles. Nearby is Hot Springs, the states top destination offering gambling, theme parks, a lively arts district and world-famous bathhouses.

Escapes! to the Branson Yacht Club

Vacationers to this lakeside resort enjoy easy access to Bransons attractions as well as to the country club amenities at nearby StoneBridge Village. Start your day with a round on StoneBridge’s Ledgestone Golf Course; spend the afternoon bass fishing or skiing along the scenic mountain coves on Table Rock Lake. Owners and guests also have an array of amenities on-site at the adjoining Rock Lane Resort. This includes two pools, tennis, a full-service marina and a lakeside restaurant/lounge.

Escapes! to StoneBridge Village

While close to all the fun and excitement of Branson, StoneBridge Villages 3,200 wooded acres offer a natural buffer to the daily hustle-and-bustle. Among the amenities enjoyed here is Ledgestone, a unique mountain layout that’s considered one of the state’s best courses. Guests at StoneBridge enjoy all the amenities at Branson Yacht Club, another Escapes! resort just three miles away on Table Rock Lake. We’re also just minutes from Branson’s famed entertainment strip, Silver Dollar City, Celebration City and Shepherd of the Hills.

Escapes! to the Gulf at Orange Beach

This Escapes! resort rests on one of the world’s most magnificent seashores. Miles of sugar white beaches reach down to the warm, blue Gulf waters, creating a seaside destination that rivals any along the Alabama/Florida coast. Look inland and you’ll find a number of attractions in Orange Beach and adjoining Gulf Shores. Theme parks, waterside restaurants, historic sites, signature golf and shopping are among the activities that bring families back year-after-year.

Transaction Fees:

No Fee For:

  • First Transaction Each Use Year
  • Checking Availability/Unfilled Requests

$20 Fee For:

  • Making/Changing a Reservation (Pursuant to Sec. 6.6 of ETC Rules and Regulations)
  • Saving Points to Subsequent Use Year
  • Borrowing Points from Subsequent Use Year
  • Canceling a Reservation by an ETC
  • Purchasing a Guest Certificate

Additional Use Fees:

Additional use fees represent costs, including housekeeping, restocking and other occupancy costs, associated with the multiple use opportunities within the Club system.

Use Fee Credit:

ETC Members receive one use fee credit each Use Year, the amount of which is equal to the additional use fee for the Unit type owned. Accordingly, ETC Members who make a Home Resort Priority reservation pay no additional use fee for that reservation, and their credit for that Unit is utilized for that year. Similarly, an ETC Member who makes a reservation in the Full Week, Split Week or Open Season reservation periods may apply any unused credit to such reservations. Unapplied use fee credits will carry forward to an ETC Member’s subsequent Use Year only if such Member saves points into that subsequent period. ETC Members who own multiple Timeshare Interests receive use fee credit for each Timeshare Interest owned.

Restrictions on Reservations using ETC Nightly Point Values:
  • Reservations for Split Weeks may only be made during either the Split Week Access Period or the Open Season Period.
  • Reservations for Partial Weeks (2-night minimum) may only be made during the Open Season Period.

ETC Point Requirements for Split Week or Partial Weeks:

To determine the number of ETC Points required to make a reservation for a Split Week or Partial Week, add the Nightly ETC Point Values, in the chart above, for the nights within the Split Week or Partial Week being requested.
